## Recommended Draft

Hello,

My name is Kristian Jackson. I wanted to share a fan-made project I have been building because *The Magnus Archives* has had a real impact on my life and creative thinking.

The project is called TMAGen. It is an unofficial fan-made writing platform inspired by *The Magnus Archives*, built around the idea that if a tool is going to generate Magnus-adjacent fiction at all, it should stay visibly grounded in the archive rather than treating the source material as invisible inspiration. The system uses published transcript material as a retrieval layer, keeps revision history and provenance attached to drafts, and lets a creator publish exact versions intentionally rather than treating output as disposable text.

The live site is here:

- `https://tmagen-web.kristian-jackson.workers.dev/`

The repository is here:

- `https://github.com/kristianjackson/tmagen`

There are currently four public showcase stories in the archive:

- `Platform Nine, No Service`
- `The Last Floor Plan`
- `The Last Weather Balloon`
- `No Tape Attached`

I want to be explicit that this is unofficial and fan-made. I am not trying to imply affiliation or speak for Rusty Quill. I built it as a project of love, and as a way of showing how much the world, structure, and atmosphere of *The Magnus Archives* have stayed with me.

If it is of interest, I would be grateful if someone on the team took a look. Even a brief reaction would mean a lot.

Best,

Kristian Jackson
